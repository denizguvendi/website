About Me

Deniz Güvendi is a licensed architect based in Turkey. She studied architecture at Istanbul Technical University (B.Arch), Cracow University of Technology (Erasmus+), and KU Leuven (M.Arch) in Ghent. Her work spans international competitions and collaborative projects across Turkey, Belgium, South Korea, and Africa.

She is the co-founder of ArchiGrad, a design initiative focused on infrastructure stacks as a foundation for architectural thinking. Her interests include adaptability, circular systems, and the interplay between nature, humans, and machines in design.
